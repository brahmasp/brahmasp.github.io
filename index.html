Brahma Pavse

Welcome! I recently completed my undergraduate and master's degrees from the University of Texas at Austin, where was I fortunate to be advised by Peter Stone. During that time, I worked on reinforcement learning (RL) problems.

I still continue to work as member of the Learning Agents Research Group (LARG). I completed my undergraduate honors thesis and master's thesis under Peter Stone. I was also a member of UTAustinVilla, our RoboCup robot soccer simulation team.
